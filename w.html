MOBILE LEGEND

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang” is a mobile MOBA. Unlike games such as DOTA2 or League of Legends (LOL), you will only need a compatible mobile phone to run the game. The game was developed by Moonton, a gaming developer located at Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, with a company life nine months. Yep, just nine months. To boot, this is only Moonton’s only video game developed and released, available for both android and iOS.
